About Us

Based in West Melbourne, Corsa Electrical Contractors Pty Ltd is a locally owned business dedicated to providing safe, professional, and efficient electrical services. From small residential jobs to commercial or industrial construction, we pride ourselves on delivering quality workmanship and reliable results every time.

Corsa Electrical Contractors is owned and operated by Adam Brock, a licensed electrician and registered electrical contractor. With extensive experience on the tools and coordinating residential, commercial and industrial construction projects, Adam ensures his projects meet deadlines, while maintaining the highest safety standards.
