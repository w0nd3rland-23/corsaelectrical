Who We Are

Based in West Melbourne, Corsa Electrical Contractors Pty Ltd is a locally owned business dedicated to providing safe, professional, and efficient electrical services throughout Melbourne and Geelong.

From small residential repairs to large-scale commercial and industrial construction projects, we pride ourselves on delivering quality workmanship and reliable results every time. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in Melbourne's electrical services industry.

Corsa Electrical Contractors is owned and operated by Adam Brock, a licensed electrician with extensive experience in residential, commercial, and industrial electrical work.
